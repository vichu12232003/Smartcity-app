About Surat

Surat is a city in the Indian state of Gujarat. It is the eighth most populous city in India, and the second-largest city in Gujarat, after Ahmedabad.

Surat SMART Ciri

Surat SMART Ciri is a program that aims to make Surat a smart city by improving its infrastructure, services, and quality of life.
